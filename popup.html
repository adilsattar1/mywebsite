Project Highlight: Sweet Treats Admin Dashboard

We developed a custom CRM for Nneka's Bakery, tailored to streamline their business operations. The CRM includes:

  • Order Management: Comprehensive dashboard for viewing, editing, and managing orders with details like shipping methods, payment options, and status updates (e.g., "Delivery Mail Sent").
  • User Management: Efficient tools for managing website users and their activities.
  • Newsletter Subscriptions: Integrated module for managing customer subscriptions and communication.
  • Website Setup: Features for managing pages, blogs, coupons, and offers.

The custom CRM empowers the bakery with a centralized system to efficiently handle their daily tasks while improving customer satisfaction. This project highlights our ability to deliver tailored solutions for unique business needs.
